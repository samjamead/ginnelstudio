The Studio

About Ginnel Studio

Ginnel Studio is a (very) small website development and copywriting agency. You can find us wedged between two houses in Grouville, Jersey.

Historically an alleyway through a terrace, at some point a wall went up the front and the back, and an extra house was squeezed in. Fast-forward 200-odd years, and that house is now home to Ginnel Studio.

Most of the time it's a one-man band (so you have to question the use of the third person here), but when the need arises we can just about fit a superstar ensemble of back-end developers, marketers, and designers.

Hello – I'm Sam, web developer, copywriter, and the hands behind Ginnel Studio.

In the last 10 years I've done some other things, too: cofounded a SaaS tech startup; lead B2B marketing at a C-Series healthcare startup; and taught physics at high-school level.

I write copy and build websites for all kinds of clients — some are very big, and some are just as small as Ginnel. This year I've worked with a marine conversation organisation, artists, engineers, and blockchain companies.

Whatever the brief, I'll find the tool for the job. I work with a wide variety of front-end platforms, from raw Javascript and CSS through to Wordpress and Squarespace.

I'm committed to helping those who are less fortunate than I am (as well as the planet). To that end I'm a member of Giving What We Can, One Percent for the Planet, and since September 2020 I've planted 850 trees with Ecologi.
